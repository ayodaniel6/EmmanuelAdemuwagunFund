Meet Emmanuel

Engineer. Technology Leader.

I am Emmanuel Ademuwagun — an Electrical Engineer and Technology Leader with over a decade of engineering delivery in Industrial Control Systems, designing, commissioning, and optimising energy infrastructure across complex industrial environments.

I provided strategic engineering and technology leadership at Arnergy, a Bill Gates–backed cleantech scale-up in Africa, and contributed to global organisations including Coca-Cola HBC and Diageo. I currently serve as Lead of Control Systems Engineering at Redlen Technologies in Victoria, BC — a Canon Inc. company acquired for $430M.

I am now focused on applying AI to Cleantech to drive scalable, sustainable impact — and recognised by SolarQuarter as a Top 40 Under 40 leader in the Middle East & Africa.
